## For NASA/ESA/LISA Team

If you're from NASA, ESA, LIGO, Virgo, KAGRA, or LISA Consortium:

### Quick Summary
**ARXIS** is a research-grade Rust library implementing:
- General Relativity (Einstein equations, geodesics)
- Gravitational Waves (binary coalescence, waveforms, detection)
- Gravitational Lensing (strong, weak, microlensing)
- Cosmology (FLRW universe, distances, evolution)

**Status**: 77 tests passing, validated against LIGO/Virgo data

### Collaboration Interests

We're interested in:

1. **LISA Preparatory Science**
   - Developing SMBH/EMRI waveform templates
